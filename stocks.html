EV-to-EBITDA

Determining valuation in relation to the stock's major competitors.

Discounted Cash Flow

Predicts the future value of current operating cash flows for companies in the Healthcare, Finance, Telecommunications, and Consumer Defense sectors.

Risk Forecasting

Using historical Monte Carlo Simulations and expected shortfall, these two measures combined will effectively assess the future risk of the stock.
